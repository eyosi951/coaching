About Our Coaching Lab

Advancing Professional Sports Coaching

Inspired by research from the Sports Coaching Lab, our team is dedicated to advancing the sports coaching profession through evidence-based methodologies and structured development programs.

We address the critical need for qualified coaching by providing comprehensive education that combines managerial expertise, socio-economic understanding, and performance optimization techniques.

Our approach is grounded in qualitative research and participatory observations, ensuring our programs meet the real-world challenges faced by coaches and athletes in today's competitive sports landscape.

Our Founding Leadership

Dr. Desiree Vardhan

Sports Coaching Specialist & Research Lead

With over 30 years of experience as an athlete, coach, and sports administrator, Dr. Vardhan brings unparalleled expertise to sports coaching development.

Her doctoral research on "Sports Coaching Realities in South Africa: An Exploratory Study" formed the foundation for our evidence-based approach to coach education and professionalization.

  • Co-authored the South African Coaching Framework
  • Introduced the ICCE Coach Developer Program in South Africa
  • Chairs the ICCE Emerging Nations Workgroup
  • Former Chef de Mission for Team South Africa
